Plan of Action

1. Boosting Exec Involvement

  • Build a smaller, solid team of dedicated members.
  • Hold monthly, informal sync-ups for planning and momentum.
  • Provide shoutouts and credit to motivate and appreciate.

2. Better Inter-Dept Coordination

  • Conduct quick planning syncs with creative, logistics, and other teams before events.
  • Utilize shared documents and calendars for clarity.
  • Ensure close collaboration during events (e.g., BOTSON, Codechella) between tech and other teams for smooth operations.

3. Positive Culture + Faculty Coordination

  • Cultivate a friendly, unforced environment for contribution.
  • Maintain strong rapport with faculty while remaining approachable for the team.
  • Lead by example: fun for the team, dependable for staff.

4. Year-Round Tech Readiness

  • Host short, engaging internal practice sessions.
  • Ensure clear, understandable documentation.
  • Encourage cross-learning; every member should pick up one new skill each year..
